alphaROM uses pretty much the same patches as PHHusson's Treble Experimentations so it's compatible with all devices supported by phh GSIs!

As you know, PHHusson patches AOSP itself by himself and provides ROMs like that. You install them as image files (Since it's a GSI), reboot and, if everything is fine, use as your daily ROM. alphaROM is basically the same. But, we have differences for sure!

  • PHHusson just does patches to make AOSP bootable and most bugless possible. But we have taken this to another dimension and make patches over AOSP to provide the features.
  • Since this project was born in early 2021, it's based on Android 11 - While PHHusson started on Android 8 and put over it.
